# 🏨 Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data to identify patterns and factors influencing booking cancellations. The goal is to uncover actionable insights that can help hotels reduce cancellations and improve revenue management.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Understand key factors affecting booking cancellations
* Identify customer behavior patterns
* Provide business recommendations to minimize revenue loss

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Source: Kaggle (Hotel Booking Demand Dataset)
* Records: ~119,000 bookings
* Features: Lead time, ADR (Average Daily Rate), market segment, customer type, etc.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas & NumPy
* Matplotlib & Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Bookings with **long lead times** have a significantly higher cancellation rate
* **City hotels** experience more cancellations compared to resort hotels
* Higher **ADR (Average Daily Rate)** is associated with increased cancellations
* Certain **market segments** (like online travel agents) show higher cancellation behavior

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Implement stricter policies or deposits for long lead-time bookings
* Offer incentives for early confirmation to reduce uncertainty
* Optimize pricing strategy for high ADR bookings
* Target reliable customer segments with personalized offers
